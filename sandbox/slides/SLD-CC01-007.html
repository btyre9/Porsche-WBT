Communication Has Direct Financial Impact

The Scenario

A customer's vehicle needs $3,500 in brake and suspension work. They're hesitant — and considering getting a second opinion at an independent shop. What happens next depends entirely on communication.

Option A

No Conversation

  • Technician doesn't explain the findings
  • Customer doesn't understand why the work is necessary
  • Customer declines and leaves for a second opinion
0 Billable Hours
VS
Option B

5-Minute Explanation

  • Technician shows the worn parts directly to the customer
  • Explains the safety and performance consequences clearly
  • Addresses cost concerns professionally alongside the service advisor
  • Customer understands the necessity — and approves the work
0 Billable Hours

The Math

5 min → 8 hrs

Your communication = Your income

Beyond This One Job

  • Customer returns for future service (lifetime value)
  • Customer refers other Porsche owners
  • Dealership success = your job security & advancement

"Better communication isn't time away from profitable work — it's an investment in MORE profitable work."
