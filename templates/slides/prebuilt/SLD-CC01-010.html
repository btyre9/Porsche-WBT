What Customers Really Want From You

Understanding this changes how you approach every customer conversation.

Customers ARE Seeking
✓
Credibility from the actual expert "I want to hear it from the person who actually knows"
✓
Transparency and unfiltered information "I want the truth about my car"
✓
Technical depth "I have questions the service advisor can't answer"
✓
Trust through direct conversation "I need to feel confident in my decision"
✓
Shared enthusiasm "I want to talk to someone who gets it"
Customers are NOT Seeking
✕
Price negotiation That's service advisor territory
✕
To catch you in a lie They're seeking honesty, not confrontation
✕
Warranty dispute resolution Management handles these situations
✕
To waste your time They respect your expertise and value your input
Key Insight

Porsche owners are often enthusiasts. They WANT to understand their vehicles. They respect technical expertise. Speaking with you is often a highlight of their service experience — not a complaint.
