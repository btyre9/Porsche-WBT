Why Brand Ambassadors Matter

Why An Ambassador?

Reason 1

Porsche Customers Have High Expectations

  • Not just for the vehicle, but for the entire experience.
  • Every interaction should reflect the premium brand promise.
  • Service experience must match purchase experience.

Reason 2

Every Interaction Matters

  • Each conversation either strengthens or weakens customer connection.
  • Small negative experiences can damage lifetime loyalty.
  • Positive interactions create lasting brand advocates.

Reason 3

You Have Unique Credibility

  • Advanced technicians possess expertise service advisors may not have.
  • Customers trust the person who actually worked on their car.
  • Technical knowledge plus professional communication is powerful.

Bottom line: Your role as Brand Ambassador is not optional. It is essential to maintaining Porsche's reputation and customer loyalty.
